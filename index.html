A Quick Summary

Welcome to the BreathFriend privacy policy. This document explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. Our goal is to be transparent and protect your privacy while we work to improve your experience with our app.

By using BreathFriend, you agree to this policy. We only collect data to make the app better and more reliable. We use services like Firebase Analytics and RevenueCat (for purchases and subscriptions). We will never sell your data.

If you have questions, you can always reach us at vortrekdev@gmail.com.

Data We Collect

To provide and improve our service, we use trusted third-party services that automatically collect certain data. Here is a breakdown of what is collected and by which service.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase to understand how you use the app, which helps us improve features and fix bugs. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

Data Collected by Firebase Analytics

Identifiers

Anonymous app-instance ID and, if permitted by you, the Android Advertising ID to calculate user metrics.

Device Information

Device type, model, OS version, and app version to optimize performance and fix bugs.

Usage Data

Session duration, features used, screens viewed, and crash reports to analyze engagement and improve stability.

Approximate Geolocation

Country and city derived from your IP address to understand regional usage patterns.

Data Processed by RevenueCat (For In-App Purchases)

RevenueCat processes purchase-related data in order to manage entitlements. We do not see or store your credit card number.

Purchase Information

Purchase history and subscription status (e.g., active, expired) to unlock features and provide support.

Identifiers

An anonymized user identifier (e.g., RevenueCat app user ID) to link purchases to your device/account without directly identifying you.

Transaction Data

Secure transaction receipts from Google Play used to validate and maintain your entitlements.
